Pizza

Description

Pizza Margherita is a super delicious and extremely simple to bake pizza without meat toppings. The recipe will come in handy for any lover of minimalism and perfectly sized ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 full cup of wheat flour - preferably type. 00 - 180 g
  • no more than half a cup of slightly warm water - up to 125 ml
  • 1 level spoon of extra-virgin olive oil
  • half a teaspoon of instant yeast - up to 5 g
  • half a flat teaspoon of salt
  • pinch of sugar
  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Knead the dough by hand or with a mixer with the yeast
  3. With your hands, stretch the dough into the shape of a pie with a diameter of about 24-25 cm.
