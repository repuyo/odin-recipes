Lasagna

Description

A delicious and super simple to make lasagna. This is my Polish version of lasagne with minced meat. I have described the recipe step by step along with supporting photos. Lazania. a fantastic option for a filling dinner.

  • simple lasagne recipe
  • super delicious dinner idea
  • a very filling and delicious everyday dish

Ingredients

  • 700 g pork shoulder
  • 2 medium onions - 200 g
  • half a bulb of garlic - 6 large cloves
  • 200 g mushrooms
  • a glass of tomato puree - 330 g
  • a larger jar of tomato paste - 200 g
  • Herbs and spices: flat spoonful each of marjoram and herbes de Provence, flat spoonful each of salt, pepper and sweet paprika
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  1. Start by making the minced meat filling
  2. Add the spices to the minced meat
  3. Peel the onions and chop into small cubes
